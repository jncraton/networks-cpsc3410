Fourier Analysis
----------------

Periodic functions can be represented as an infinite series of sines and cosines.

---

$$g(t) = {1 \over 2}c + \sum_{n=1}^{\infty}{a_n \sin{2\pi n f t}} + \sum_{n=1}^{\infty}{b_n \cos{2\pi n f t}}$$
